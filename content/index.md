# 🧬 Welcome to the Qubit Zoo

The **Qubit Zoo** is a living, interlinked knowledge base of quantum computing hardware — every qubit modality, architecture, and gate primitive we know about, organized as a [Zettelkasten](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zettelkasten).
