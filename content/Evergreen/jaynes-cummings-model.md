The Jaynes-Cummings model describes coherent exchange between a two-level system and a single bosonic mode:

$$H = \hbar\omega_r a^\dagger a + \frac{\hbar\omega_q}{2}\sigma_z + \hbar g(a^\dagger\sigma_- + a\sigma_+)$$

It predicts vacuum Rabi splitting and underpins circuit-QED readout and coupling architectures.
