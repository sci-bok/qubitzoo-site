# Quantum Hardware

Umbrella concept encompassing all physical implementations of quantum bits (qubits) and the supporting infrastructure for quantum information processing.

Quantum hardware spans multiple technology families:
- **[[superconducting-moc|Superconducting]]** — Josephson-junction-based circuits (transmon, fluxonium, flux qubit, etc.)
- **[[trapped-ion-moc|Trapped Ion]]** — Individual ions confined in electromagnetic traps
- **[[semiconducting-moc|Semiconducting]]** — Spin qubits in quantum dots (Si, Ge, GaAs)
- **[[photonic-moc|Photonic]]** — Single photons or bosonic modes
- **[[topological-moc|Topological]]** — Majorana zero modes and related non-Abelian anyons
- **[[neutral-atom-moc|Neutral Atom]]** — Atoms in optical tweezers/lattices

See the [[qubit-zoo-index-moc|Qubit Zoo]] for a comprehensive catalog of qubit implementations.
