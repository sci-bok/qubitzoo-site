## Citation

F. Wang, K. Lu, F. Bao et al., "High-Coherence Fluxonium Qubit with Wafer-Scale Overlap Josephson Junctions," [Phys. Rev. Applied **23**, 044064 (2025)](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevApplied.23.044064)

## Key Results

- Demonstrated $T_1 > 1\,\text{ms}$ fluxonium qubits at the flux-frustration point.
- Used an overlap Josephson junction fabrication process on 2-inch (50.8 mm) wafers.
- Achieved nearly 100% JJ yield with $<5\%$ variation for phase-slip junctions and $<2\%$ for full arrays.
- Compact superinductor array design minimizes dielectric loss and flux noise.
- Key step toward CMOS-compatible, scalable fabrication of high-coherence fluxonium processors.
