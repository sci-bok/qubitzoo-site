Demonstrates single-qubit gate fidelity of 99.8% and two-qubit gate fidelity of 99.5% in a Si/SiGe double quantum dot, both above the surface code threshold. Uses EDSR with a micromagnet and exchange-based CZ gates.

## Key Results
- 1Q gate fidelity: 99.8% (randomized benchmarking)
- 2Q CZ gate fidelity: 99.5%
- Platform: ²⁸Si/SiGe double quantum dot
- Gate time: ~100 ns (1Q), ~50 ns (2Q)
