Demonstrates universal quantum logic on nuclear spin qubits of ¹⁷¹Yb atoms in optical tweezers. The nuclear spin (I=1/2) provides a first-order magnetic-field-insensitive clock qubit with T₂ > 10 seconds, while Rydberg-mediated gates enable two-qubit entanglement.

## Key Results
- Nuclear spin qubit: I=1/2 of ¹⁷¹Yb (clock states)
- T₂ > 10 s (spin echo, magnetic field insensitive)
- Single-qubit gate fidelity: 99.96%
- Two-qubit gate via Rydberg blockade
