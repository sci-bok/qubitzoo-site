Demonstration of extended coherence times exceeding 1 second on the hyperfine clock transition of optically trapped ⁸⁷Rb atoms, establishing the coherence benchmark for neutral atom qubits.

## Key Results
- Achieved Ramsey coherence time T₂ ~ 1 s on the ⁸⁷Rb hyperfine clock transition (|F=1, m_F=0⟩ ↔ |F=2, m_F=0⟩)
- Used magic-wavelength optical trapping to minimize differential light shifts
- Demonstrated that hyperfine ground-state encoding provides long coherence times suitable for quantum information processing
