Proposes using the nuclear spin of ³¹P donor atoms in isotopically pure ²⁸Si as qubits, with hyperfine interaction to donor electron spins mediating two-qubit gates via gate-controlled exchange coupling. A foundational vision for silicon quantum computing.

## Key Results
- Qubit: nuclear spin of ³¹P in ²⁸Si (I = 1/2)
- Single-qubit gates via NMR with A-gate tuning of hyperfine coupling
- Two-qubit gates via J-gate control of exchange between donor electrons
- Predicted coherence: nuclear T₂ > seconds in enriched ²⁸Si
