Proposes and demonstrates the hybrid qubit: a three-electron qubit in a double quantum dot that combines the speed of charge qubits with the coherence of spin qubits. Uses a (2,1)→(1,2) charge transition where spin-orbit-like mixing creates a sweet spot.

## Key Results
- Gate times ~100 ps (charge-like speed)
- T₂* ~ 10 ns at sweet spot
- Three electrons in double quantum dot (not triple dot)
- Combines fast manipulation with improved dephasing protection
