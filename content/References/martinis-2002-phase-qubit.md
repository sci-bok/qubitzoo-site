First demonstration of coherent Rabi oscillations in a current-biased Josephson junction (phase qubit). The qubit states are the two lowest energy levels in the tilted washboard potential of a large-area junction.

## Key Results
- Coherent Rabi oscillations with T_Rabi ~ 20 ns
- Phase qubit: |0⟩ and |1⟩ are levels in anharmonic washboard potential
- Readout via tunneling: |1⟩ tunnels to voltage state, |0⟩ stays trapped
- Junction area: ~10 μm² (large by modern standards)
