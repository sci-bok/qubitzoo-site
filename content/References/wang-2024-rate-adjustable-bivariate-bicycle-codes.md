Presents a practically oriented bivariate-bicycle code construction with adjustable code rates for finite-size quantum error-correction studies.

## Key Results
- Studies bivariate-bicycle codes as an important finite-size qLDPC family rather than only asymptotic constructions.
- Emphasizes rate-adjustable instances, making hardware-resource tradeoffs more explicit.
- Serves as a concrete bridge between qLDPC theory and layout/scheduling work on realistic hardware.
