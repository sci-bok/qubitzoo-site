First proposal and demonstration of gatemonium: a fluxonium-like qubit where the large Josephson junction is replaced by a gate-tunable semiconductor junction, combining fluxonium's noise protection with gatemon's voltage tunability. This enables flux-free, all-electrical frequency control of a superinductance-shunted qubit.
