Demonstrates beyond-break-even encoded benchmarks with 48 to 94 logical qubits on the 98-qubit Quantinuum Helios trapped-ion processor using high-rate iceberg codes.

## Key Results
- 48 to 94 logical qubits on Helios
- Beyond-break-even encoded performance in fault-tolerant and partially fault-tolerant benchmarks
- High-rate iceberg QED/QEC codes on a trapped-ion processor with long-range connectivity
