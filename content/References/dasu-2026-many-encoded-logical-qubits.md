## Citation

A. Dasu et al., "Many encoded logical qubits on a versatile atomic-register quantum processor," [arXiv:2603.14292 (2026)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.14292)

## Key Results

- Demonstrated 48 to 94 logical qubits on the Quantinuum Helios trapped-ion processor.
- Used quantum LDPC-style iceberg code constructions on a dual-species Ba/Yb trapped-ion platform.
- Showed large-scale encoded-state preparation on a commercial atomic-register processor.
