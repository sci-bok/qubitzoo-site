AbstractWe present a protected superconducting qubit based on an effective circuit element that only allows pairs of Cooper pairs to tunnel.

## Key Results
- These dynamics give rise to a nearly degenerate ground state manifold indexed by the parity of tunneled Cooper pairs.
- We show that, when the circuit element is shunted by a large capacitance, this manifold can be used as a logical qubit that we expect to be insensitive to multiple relaxation and dephasing mechanisms.
