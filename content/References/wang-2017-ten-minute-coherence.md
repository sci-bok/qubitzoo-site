Demonstrates single-qubit quantum memory with coherence time exceeding 10 minutes (>600 s) in ¹⁷¹Yb⁺ using sympathetic cooling with ¹³⁸Ba⁺ ions. The long coherence is achieved by operating on the ¹⁷¹Yb⁺ hyperfine clock transition, which is first-order insensitive to magnetic field fluctuations.

## Key Results
- T₂ > 600 s (10 minutes) for single ¹⁷¹Yb⁺ qubit
- Sympathetic cooling with ¹³⁸Ba⁺ to suppress motional heating
- Hyperfine clock transition encoding for magnetic field insensitivity
