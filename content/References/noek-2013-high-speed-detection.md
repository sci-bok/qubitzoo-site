Demonstrates high-speed, high-fidelity detection of an atomic hyperfine qubit using electron shelving, achieving 99.93% SPAM fidelity.

## Key Results
- Achieved 99.93% SPAM fidelity for ytterbium hyperfine qubit
- Used background-suppressed fluorescence detection for high-speed readout
