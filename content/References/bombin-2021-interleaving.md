Proposes the interleaving technique for photonic quantum computing, enabling fault-tolerant computation by weaving together small entangled resource states into a topological code using only local fusion operations and classical feedforward. Key enabling architecture for PsiQuantum-style photonic quantum computers.

## Key Results
- Interleaving reduces hardware requirements by reusing optical components across time steps
- Compatible with ballistic (non-adaptive) photonic circuits
- Thresholds comparable to bulk topological codes when combined with fusion-based approaches
