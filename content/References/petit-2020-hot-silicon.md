Demonstrates universal quantum logic with silicon spin qubits at 1.1 K — more than 50× warmer than typical operating temperatures. Shows that silicon qubits can operate above 1 K with only modest fidelity reduction, dramatically easing cryogenic infrastructure requirements for large-scale systems.

## Key Results
- Two-qubit gate operation at 1.1 K
- CZ gate fidelity: 98.0% at 1.1 K (vs 99.3% at base temperature)
- Single-qubit fidelity: 99.3% at 1.1 K
- Platform: Si/SiGe double quantum dot
