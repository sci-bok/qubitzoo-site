Demonstrates spin coherence exceeding 10 ms for a single SiV⁻ center in diamond at millikelvin temperatures, using dynamical decoupling sequences. Achieves single-shot spin readout with >89% fidelity via resonance fluorescence.

## Key Results
- T₂ (CPMG): 13 ms at ~100 mK
- Single-shot readout fidelity: 89%
- Spin relaxation T₁ > 1 s at 100 mK
- Platform: SiV⁻ in bulk diamond, dilution refrigerator
