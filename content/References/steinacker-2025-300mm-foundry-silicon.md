First demonstration that industrially fabricated silicon quantum dot spin qubits on a standard 300mm CMOS foundry line (imec) exceed the fault-tolerance threshold across all qubit operations. Achieves SPAM fidelity >99.9% and gate fidelities >99% in isotopically enriched ²⁸Si, bridging the gap between academic "hero" devices and industrial-scale fabrication.

## Key Results
- SPAM fidelity: >99.9%
- 1Q gate fidelity: >99%
- 2Q gate fidelity: >99%
- T₁: up to 9.5 s
- T₂(Hahn echo): 1.9 ms
- Platform: 300mm Si-MOS, isotopically enriched ²⁸Si
- Fabricated at imec on standard CMOS line
