## Summary

Comprehensive review establishing the theoretical framework for quantum information processing with continuous variables. Covers quantum teleportation, entanglement, quantum cryptography, and quantum computation using the quadrature amplitudes of the electromagnetic field. Foundational reference for the CV quantum information field.
