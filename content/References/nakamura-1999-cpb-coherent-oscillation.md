First observation of coherent quantum oscillations in a superconducting circuit (Cooper pair box). This is the founding experiment of superconducting quantum computing — demonstrated that macroscopic electrical circuits can exhibit quantum coherence.

## Key Results
- Coherent Rabi oscillations between charge states of a CPB
- Oscillation period ~0.15 ns, decay time ~2 ns
- Operated at charge degeneracy point
- Established superconducting circuits as a viable qubit platform
