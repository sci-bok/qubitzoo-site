Key 3D circuit-QED cavity paper establishing millisecond-scale coherence in microwave memories.

## Key Results
- Demonstrates millisecond coherence in superconducting cavity memories within circuit QED.
- Provides the long-lived bosonic hardware substrate later used for cat, binomial, and GKP encodings.
- Helps separate cavity coherence from the shorter-lived ancilla transmon dynamics.
