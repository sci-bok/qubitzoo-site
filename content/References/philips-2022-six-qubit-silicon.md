Demonstrates universal control of a six-qubit quantum processor in a ²⁸Si/SiGe linear array with ~90 nm qubit pitch. Achieves simultaneous high fidelities for initialization, single-qubit gates, two-qubit gates, and readout through Hamiltonian engineering and efficient background calibration.

## Key Results
- 6-qubit linear array with nearest-neighbor exchange coupling
- Universal gate set with simultaneous high fidelities
- Qubit pitch: ~90 nm
- Platform: ²⁸Si/SiGe gate-defined quantum dots
