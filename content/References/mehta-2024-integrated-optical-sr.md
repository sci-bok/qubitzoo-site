## Summary

Demonstrates chip-scale photonic integrated circuit delivering a 674 nm Brillouin laser stabilized to the $^{88}\text{Sr}^+$ quadrupole transition. Achieves 6 kHz laser linewidth, 60 μs Ramsey coherence time, and 99% SPAM fidelity with Rabi oscillations demonstrated entirely without bulk free-space optics. Represents a major step toward fully integrated trapped-ion quantum processors.
