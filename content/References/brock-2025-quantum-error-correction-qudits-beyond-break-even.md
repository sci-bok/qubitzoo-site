Extends bosonic quantum error correction beyond qubits to higher-dimensional GKP-encoded qudits.

## Key Results
- Demonstrates beyond-break-even quantum error correction for bosonic qutrits and ququarts.
- Reports logical gains of 1.82 ± 0.03 for qutrits and 1.87 ± 0.03 for ququarts.
- Shows that higher-dimensional encoding inside a single oscillator can improve hardware efficiency without abandoning error correction.
