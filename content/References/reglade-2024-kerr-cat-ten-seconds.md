Demonstrates quantum control of a superconducting cat qubit while preserving its bias-protected dynamics, pushing the protected bit-flip time beyond 10 seconds. This is the clearest hardware milestone to date for a controllable cat qubit with macroscopic suppression of bit-flip errors.

## Key Results
- Bit-flip time exceeding 10 s
- Phase-flip time above 490 ns in the protected-control regime
- Roughly four orders of magnitude improvement over earlier cat-qubit bit-flip benchmarks
- Controlled preparation, imaging, and phase manipulation of cat-state superpositions without destroying protection
