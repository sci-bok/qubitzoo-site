Proposes and analyzes the cos(2φ) qubit (bifluxon), a protected superconducting qubit where both charge and flux matrix elements between qubit states are exponentially suppressed. The potential energy is π-periodic in the superconducting phase, creating protected degeneracy.

## Key Results
- Simultaneous protection against charge and flux noise
- Exponential suppression of both bit-flip and phase-flip matrix elements
- Requires superinductance + junction pair with controlled asymmetry
- Predicted T₁ enhancement: 10²–10⁴× over transmon
