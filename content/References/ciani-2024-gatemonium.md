# Gatemonium

**Ciani et al. (2024)** — [arXiv:2406.09002](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.09002)

## Summary

Introduces the gatemonium — a superconducting qubit that combines features of the gatemon (gate-tunable semiconductor Josephson junction) with the fluxonium-style inductive shunt. The semiconductor weak link replaces the traditional tunnel junction, enabling voltage-tunable $E_J$ while the superinductance provides charge noise protection.

## Key Innovation

- **Gatemon**: Josephson junction replaced by semiconductor nanowire weak link → $E_J$ tuned by gate voltage instead of magnetic flux
- **Gatemonium**: Adds superinductance shunt (like fluxonium) → combines voltage tunability with charge insensitivity
- Potential path to flux-free, all-electrical superconducting qubit control
