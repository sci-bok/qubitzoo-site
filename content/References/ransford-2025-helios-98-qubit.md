Describes Quantinuum Helios, a 98-qubit QCCD trapped-ion processor using $^{137}$Ba$^+$ hyperfine qubits with $^{171}$Yb$^+$ sympathetic cooling, all-to-all connectivity via a rotatable storage ring, and average gate/SPAM infidelities of $2.5\times10^{-5}$, $7.9\times10^{-4}$, and $4.8\times10^{-4}$ respectively.

## Key Results
- 98-qubit trapped-ion QCCD processor
- $^{137}$Ba$^+$ data qubits with $^{171}$Yb$^+$ sympathetic cooling
- Average 1Q infidelity $2.5(1)\times10^{-5}$, 2Q infidelity $7.9(2)\times10^{-4}$, SPAM infidelity $4.8(6)\times10^{-4}$
- Rotatable ion-storage ring with junction-enabled all-to-all connectivity
