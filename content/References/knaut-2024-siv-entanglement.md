Demonstrates entanglement between two SiV⁻ quantum memory nodes connected by 35 km of deployed Boston-area telecom fiber via quantum frequency conversion. First demonstration of memory-enhanced quantum communication over a metropolitan-scale fiber network.

## Key Results
- Remote entanglement fidelity: 94%
- Distance: 35 km deployed fiber (Boston)
- Quantum frequency conversion: 737 nm → 1350 nm telecom band
- Entanglement rate: ~100 per hour
