Demonstrates a two-qubit gate fidelity of 99.9(1)% for ⁴³Ca⁺ hyperfine qubits using a light-shift gate, the highest reported for any qubit platform at time of publication. Establishes trapped ions as the fidelity benchmark for two-qubit operations.

## Key Results
- Two-qubit gate fidelity: 99.9(1)% (randomized benchmarking)
- Uses a geometric phase gate driven by off-resonant laser beams
- Gate time: ~100 μs
- Platform: ⁴³Ca⁺ ions in a macroscopic linear Paul trap
