## Summary

Demonstrates deterministic quantum state transfer and entanglement between two remote superconducting cavity quantum memories using shaped microwave photons. Average transfer fidelity of 76%, improved to 87% with error heralding (success probability 0.87). Uses parametrically controlled emission and absorption of microwave photons via transmon intermediaries. Demonstrated in both single- and multi-photon encodings.
