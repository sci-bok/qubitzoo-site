Characterizes modern single-shot readout and measurement back-action in a tantalum high-coherence fluxonium device.

## Key Results
- Reports 97.8% ± 0.4% assignment fidelity with a Josephson parametric amplifier.
- Reports 99.0% ± 0.3% QND fidelity and directly studies measurement-induced state mixing.
- Important because readout, not bare coherence, has often been the practical bottleneck for fluxonium.
