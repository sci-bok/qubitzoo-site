Characterizes high-fidelity QND readout and measurement back-action in a tantalum-based high-coherence fluxonium device.

## Key Results
- Establishes a modern readout milestone for fluxonium, directly addressing one of the platform's historical weak points.
- Quantifies measurement back-action rather than reporting readout fidelity alone.
- Strengthens the case that protected fluxonium coherence can coexist with practical measurement.
