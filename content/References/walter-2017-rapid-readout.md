Demonstrates rapid, high-fidelity dispersive readout of a superconducting transmon qubit using a Purcell-filtered readout resonator and quantum-limited Josephson parametric amplifier. Achieves 99.6% single-shot readout fidelity in 48 ns — fast enough for real-time feedback in QEC.

## Key Results
- Readout fidelity: 99.6% in 48 ns integration time
- 99.2% fidelity in 24 ns
- Uses JPA (Josephson parametric amplifier) for quantum-limited amplification
- Purcell filter prevents readout-induced qubit decay
