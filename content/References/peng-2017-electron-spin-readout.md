## Summary

Proposes a scheme for reading out the spin state of a single electron qubit in a surface Paul trap using oscillating magnetic field gradients. The all-electronic readout protocol involves cooling, driving, amplification, and detection of the electron's motion. Predicts readout fidelity of 99.7% in 25 μs under realistic parameters, performing similarly to fluorescence detection in ion traps.
