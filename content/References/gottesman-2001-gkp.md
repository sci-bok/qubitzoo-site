Introduces the GKP (Gottesman-Kitaev-Preskill) code: encoding a qubit in the continuous Hilbert space of a harmonic oscillator using grid states in phase space. GKP states are simultaneous +1 eigenstates of displaced position and momentum operators, forming a lattice in phase space that can correct small displacement errors.

## Key Results
- Qubit encoded in position/momentum grid states of an oscillator
- Corrects arbitrary small displacement errors in phase space
- Stabilizers: $S_q = e^{2i\sqrt{\pi}\hat{q}}$ and $S_p = e^{-2i\sqrt{\pi}\hat{p}}$
- Concatenation with surface code yields very high thresholds (~10% displacement error)
