Pushes fluxonium coherence into a fabrication process aimed at wafer-scale uniformity rather than isolated hero devices.

## Key Results
- Reports fluxonium qubits with coherence exceeding 1 ms in a wafer-scale fabrication flow.
- Frames coherence as a manufacturability problem, not only a single-device optimization problem.
- Supports the claim that fluxonium can be evaluated as a scalable platform rather than just a laboratory curiosity.
