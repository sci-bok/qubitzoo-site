Pushes fluxonium coherence into a fabrication flow aimed at wafer-scale uniformity instead of isolated hero devices.

## Key Results
- Reports fluxonium qubits with $T_1 > 1$ ms in a nearly 100%-yield overlap-process flow.
- Achieves less than 5% variation for the phase-slip junction and less than 2% for the full junction array across a 2-inch wafer.
- Strengthens the case that fluxonium can be judged as a scalable hardware platform, not only as a boutique protected qubit.
