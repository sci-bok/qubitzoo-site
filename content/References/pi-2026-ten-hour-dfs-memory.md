Reports a decoherence-free trapped-ion clock-qubit memory with coherence exceeding ten hours using a Yb-Ba-Yb chain, with an exponential-fit coherence time of $(3.77 \pm 1.09) \times 10^4$ s.

## Key Results
- Coherence exceeding 10 hours in a decoherence-free trapped-ion clock qubit
- Uses $^{171}$Yb$^+$ ion pairs sympathetically cooled by $^{138}$Ba$^+$
- Passive rejection of microwave-phase noise and common-mode magnetic-field fluctuations via DFS encoding
