## Summary

Demonstrates control and single-shot readout of a single $^{171}\text{Yb}^{3+}$ ion in a YVO₄ nanophotonic cavity. Achieves spin coherence $T_2 > 30\,\text{ms}$ for a cavity-coupled ion and single-shot readout fidelity >95%. Optical and spin transitions are first-order insensitive to magnetic field fluctuations. Establishes the rare-earth-ion–nanophotonic-cavity platform for quantum networking.
