First decisive circuit-QED experiment showing strong coupling between a superconducting qubit and a microwave resonator on chip.

## Key Results
- Observes vacuum-Rabi splitting for a Cooper-pair box coupled to a coplanar-waveguide resonator.
- Validates the central Blais et al. 2004 proposal in an actual superconducting circuit.
- Establishes circuit QED as a practical route to dispersive readout and cavity-mediated coupling in superconducting processors.
