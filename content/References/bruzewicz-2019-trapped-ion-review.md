## Summary

Comprehensive review of trapped-ion quantum computing covering the basics of ion qubits (including $^{9}\text{Be}^+$, $^{40}\text{Ca}^+$, $^{43}\text{Ca}^+$, $^{88}\text{Sr}^+$, $^{171}\text{Yb}^+$, $^{137}\text{Ba}^+$), their strengths and limitations, gate implementations, decoherence mechanisms, and scaling challenges. Documents the state of the field as of 2019 with extensive bibliography of 358 references.
