## Citation

J. M. Elzerman, R. Hanson, L. H. Willems van Beveren, B. Witkamp, L. M. K. Vandersypen, and L. P. Kouwenhoven, "Single-shot read-out of an individual electron spin in a quantum dot," [Nature **430**, 431 (2004)](https://doi.org/10.1038/nature02693)

## Key Results

- First fully electrical single-shot readout of an individual electron spin in a semiconductor quantum dot.
- Demonstrated spin-to-charge conversion via energy-selective tunneling: spin-up tunnels to reservoir, spin-down stays.
- Charge detection via quantum point contact (QPC) discriminates the resulting charge state.
- Single-shot visibility >80% for two-electron spin states.
- Measured very long triplet-to-singlet relaxation times up to several milliseconds.
- Foundational technique for all modern semiconductor spin qubit readout.
