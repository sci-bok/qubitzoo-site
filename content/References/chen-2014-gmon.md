Introduces the gmon qubit: an Xmon transmon with an integrated tunable coupler based on a shared inductance loop. Enables fast switching between zero and strong coupling, allowing high-fidelity two-qubit gates without residual ZZ interaction.

## Key Results
- Tunable coupling: 0 to 30 MHz, switchable in ~10 ns
- Integrated coupler (no separate coupler qubit needed)
- Two-qubit iSWAP gate time: ~12 ns
- Architecture used in Google's quantum supremacy processor
