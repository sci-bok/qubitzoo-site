# Quantum Memory with Millisecond Coherence in Circuit QED

**Reagor et al., PRB 94, 014506 (2016)** — [arXiv:1508.05882](https://arxiv.org/abs/1508.05882)

## Summary

Demonstrates superconducting 3D microwave cavities with single-photon lifetimes exceeding 1 millisecond, coupled to transmon qubits for quantum control. These high-Q cavities serve as quantum memories and as the physical substrate for bosonic error-correcting codes (cat codes, binomial codes, GKP codes).

## Key Results

- Cavity $T_1 > 1$ ms (quality factor $Q > 10^7$)
- Achieved via careful materials engineering (high-purity aluminum, seam minimization)
- Transmon qubit dispersively coupled for state preparation, manipulation, and readout
- Demonstrated quantum memory operations preserving coherence over ms timescales
