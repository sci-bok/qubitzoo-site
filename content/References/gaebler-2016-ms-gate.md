Demonstrates a universal gate set for ⁹Be⁺ hyperfine qubits with single-qubit gate fidelity of 99.9999% and two-qubit Mølmer-Sørensen gate fidelity of 99.9(1)%, both characterized by randomized benchmarking. Establishes the highest single-qubit gate fidelity for any platform.

## Key Results
- 1Q gate fidelity: 99.9999(1)% (randomized benchmarking)
- 2Q MS gate fidelity: 99.9(1)%
- Platform: ⁹Be⁺ hyperfine qubits in surface-electrode trap
- Error dominated by spontaneous emission from Raman beams
