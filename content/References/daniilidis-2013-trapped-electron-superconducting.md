## Summary

Proposes interfacing trapped electrons with superconducting electronics for quantum information processing. Describes a parametric frequency conversion scheme using geometric nonlinearities in the trapping potential to coherently transfer quantum information between electron motional states and superconducting resonators. Establishes the theoretical basis for all-electronic trapped-electron quantum computing.
