First demonstration of a four-qubit quantum processor in germanium (Ge/SiGe) hole spin qubits. Demonstrates universal quantum logic including single-qubit gates, two-qubit CNOT, and four-qubit GHZ state preparation. Germanium's strong spin-orbit coupling enables all-electrical control without micromagnets.

## Key Results
- 4 hole spin qubits in a 2×2 Ge/SiGe quantum dot array
- Single-qubit fidelity: 99.3% (randomized benchmarking)
- Two-qubit CZ gate via exchange interaction
- All-electrical control (no micromagnets or ESR — spin-orbit-driven EDSR)
- Operated at ~20 mK
