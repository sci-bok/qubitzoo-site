Introduces integer fluxonium, a zero-field fluxonium variant that keeps strong coherence while moving the qubit frequency into a more control-friendly range.

## Key Results
- Demonstrates a zero-flux fluxonium operating point, easing control relative to half-flux-biased heavy-fluxonium devices.
- Reports a qubit frequency near 4 GHz, Ramsey coherence beyond 100 μs, and average Clifford fidelity above 99.9%.
- Shows that fluxonium-style protection can coexist with a more conventional microwave-control regime.
