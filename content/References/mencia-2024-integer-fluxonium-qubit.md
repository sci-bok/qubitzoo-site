Introduces integer fluxonium, a fluxonium variant operated at zero external flux while retaining strong coherence and large anharmonicity.

## Key Results
- Demonstrates a zero-flux fluxonium operating point, easing control relative to half-flux biased heavy-fluxonium devices.
- Reports GHz-scale qubit frequency, quality factor near 10^6, and Ramsey coherence beyond 1 ms.
- Shows that fluxonium-style protection can coexist with a control-friendly operating point.
- Makes fluxonium more plausible as a scalable architecture instead of only a protected-memory niche.
