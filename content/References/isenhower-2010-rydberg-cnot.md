First demonstration of a CNOT gate using neutral atoms via Rydberg blockade interactions between atoms held in optical traps separated by >8 μm.

## Key Results
- Demonstrated controlled-NOT gate between two individually trapped ⁸⁷Rb atoms using Rydberg blockade
- Measured CNOT fidelities of F = 0.73 and 0.72 using two different gate protocols
- Showed by measurement of parity oscillations that the gate generates two-atom states with fidelity at the threshold for entanglement
- Established the viability of Rydberg interactions for multi-qubit neutral atom quantum computing
