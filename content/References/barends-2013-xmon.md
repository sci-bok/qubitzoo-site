Introduces the Xmon: a planar transmon variant with a cross-shaped capacitor geometry enabling scalable 2D arrays with individual qubit addressability and nearest-neighbor coupling. The Xmon design became the foundation for Google's Sycamore processor and the quantum supremacy experiment.

## Key Results
- T₁ > 40 μs, T₂* > 20 μs
- Cross-shaped capacitor with 4 coupling ports
- Scalable 2D tiling with individual XY and Z control
- Single-qubit gate fidelity >99.9%, two-qubit CZ >99.4%
